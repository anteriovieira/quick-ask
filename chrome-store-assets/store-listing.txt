Title: Quick Ask - AI Text Assistant

Short Description:
Instantly ask AI about any selected text on the web. Explain, summarize, translate, and more with a single click.

Long Description:
Quick Ask is your intelligent browsing companion. Select any text on any webpage and instantly get answers, explanations, summaries, or translations using advanced AI.

Features:
- **Instant Answers**: Just select text and click "Ask AI" to get immediate insights.
- **Quick Actions**: One-click buttons to Explain, Summarize, Translate, Fix Typos, and more.
- **Custom Actions**: Create your own reusable prompts for repetitive tasks.
- **Contextual Chat**: Have a follow-up conversation with the AI about the selected text.
- **Side Panel Interface**: A seamless experience that doesn't block your view.
- **Customizable System Prompt**: Tailor the AI's personality and behavior to your needs.
- **Secure**: Uses your own OpenAI API key.

Boost your productivity and understanding while browsing the web with Quick Ask.
